## About Me

My name is Peizhi Shi. I am a [research fellow](https://pure.hud.ac.uk/en/persons/peizhi-shi) at the EPSRC future advanced metrology hub in the University of Huddersfield. I do research in machine learning in the math group.

I received the PhD in computer science from the University of Manchester in 2019 under the supervision of [Dr. Ke Chen](http://www.cs.man.ac.uk/~kechen), the master's degree in software engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2013, and the bachelor's degree in computer science from the Guilin University of Electronic Technology in 2010.


My current research interests include machine learning and its applications in intelligent system development. For details, please visit my [Publication](publications.html) page.
